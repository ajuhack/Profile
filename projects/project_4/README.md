Second Line of Defence for US Consumer Banking fraud across Debit Card, Credit Card, Cheque, EMM, and Wire.

## Role
Fraud Risk Strategy — Consumer & Business Banking

## Duration
May 2016 – Apr 2019

## Key Achievements
- –15% digital fraud exposure reduction
- $1M/year reporting cost savings via Tableau transition
- Created fraud-attack-lifecycle forecasting methodology
- Partnered with Visa generating $1.0–1.5M/year in recoveries
- BCBS and US federal regulatory reporting

## Technologies
- Tableau, Python, SQL, SAS, Visa APIs, Statistical Forecasting

## Impact
- Rebuilt fraud MI and forecasting for US Consumer Banking
- Standard methodology adopted across the line of business
- Significant cost and loss reduction
