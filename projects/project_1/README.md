# Project 1: L&T Finance - Head of Risk Analytics

## Overview
Standing up an enterprise-grade risk analytics function at one of India's largest NBFCs (~USD 11B retail book).

## Role
Head of Risk Analytics (Retail Risk & RCU)

## Duration
Dec 2023 – Present

## Key Achievements
- Built central risk-analytics capability (data-mart, dashboards, governance, BI team)
- ML-based fraud detection improving early-detection rates by 20%
- Ensemble risk model spanning dealer performance, field-sales, and geographic scorecards
- BCBS 239 aligned data pipelines and reporting

## Technologies
- Python, SAS, GCP, Azure, Tableau, Power BI, ML/AI

## Impact
- Single source of truth for audit and regulators
- Tangible reduction in delinquencies
- Executive governance forums with CRO, CIO, and business heads
